Listen, Learn, Improve

Get actionable insights with Lumioli’s Feedback Collector. It asks smart questions at the right time and turns customer opinions into better business decisions.

Benefits

  • Automated surveys triggered post-purchase or interaction
  • Gathers qualitative and quantitative feedback
  • Detects satisfaction trends and pain points

How It Works

The bot engages your customers through web, email, or SMS with customized questions. It analyzes responses using sentiment detection and presents a dashboard with key insights for your team to act on.

FAQs

What type of questions can it ask?

You can set open-ended, multiple choice, rating scales, and more.

Will I be notified of negative feedback?

Yes, critical responses are flagged and can trigger alerts or follow-up actions.

Can I track response trends over time?

Absolutely. You’ll get historical data, charts, and export options.
